## Module Structure: What You'll Learn

This module guides you through:

1. **Self-Assessment Quiz**: Evaluate your readiness for the course
2. **Prerequisite Knowledge**: Python, Linux, and math fundamentals
3. **Learning Paths**: Three options tailored to your resources and goals
4. **Environment Setup**: Installation and configuration guides
5. **Support Resources**: Where to find help when you need it

---

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

1. **Assess your readiness** for the course by identifying gaps in programming, Linux, and robotics knowledge
2. **Understand prerequisite knowledge and skills** required before proceeding to Module 1
3. **Choose a learning path** aligned with your resources (simulation-only, edge hardware, or full physical)
4. **Configure your development environment** for Python, ROS 2, and robotics software
5. **Access support resources** and understand how to troubleshoot common setup issues
